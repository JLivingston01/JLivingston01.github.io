Machine Learning Course

This is a course on Machine Learning and its professional applications. I developed these materials following 4 years as an Adjunct Associate Faculty at the School of Professional Studies at Columbia University to address what I see as gaps between how Machine Learning is taught, and how it is practiced in industry.
