Jason Livingston's Biography

I am a 28 year old data scientist, based in New York City. In my career I have spent time as a Manager of Marketing Analytics, a marketing Data Scientist and Analyst focusing on Mixed Media Modeling, building other predictive models, sourcing data, web scraping and API integration, natural language processing and sentiment analysis, other analyses related to determining the suggess of TV and Digital advertising.

I have also worked as a consulting data scientist where I have concentrated on insurance risk-modeling, data sourcing and preprocessing, website tagging and performance analytics, serving in supporting roles and as a technical lead.

I have a BS in Mathematics from Concordia University Texas, where I was a goalkeeper on the men's soccer team. I also hold an MS in Applied Analytics from Columbia University, where I served as the director of technology for the Applied Analytics Club.

In my free time I still enjoy playing soccer here in NYC, I skateboard when I have the chance and I love playing the guitar.
