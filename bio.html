About Jason Livingston

Previous Next


Jason Livingston is a Senior Data Scientist based in New York City. He has served as a Manager of Marketing Analytics, a marketing Data Scientist and Analyst focusing in Mixed Media Modeling, predictive sales modeling, the sourcing and scraping of valuable data and natural language processing, with the goal of measuring the success of advertising campaigns and optimizing.

He has also worked as a consulting Data Scientist with experience in the insurance industry, investment banking, website tagging and performance analytics, serving in supporting roles and as a technical lead.

Jason graduated Summa Cum Laude with a BS in Mathematics from Concordia University Texas, where he is an inductee into the National College Athlete Honor Society as a goalkeeper on the Men's Soccer team. He holds his MS in Applied Analytics from Columbia University, where he graduated with the Distinguished Student Leader honor, having served as the Director of Technology for the Applied Analytics Club. Jason serves as an Adjunct Associate Faculty Member in Columbia University's Applied Analytics program, teaching courses in Machine Learning: Concepts and Applications.
